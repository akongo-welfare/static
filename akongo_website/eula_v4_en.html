Article 4 - Description and Use of Services

4.1 AKONGO WELFARE MANAGER allows the user to benefit from welfare analysis services provided by the AKONGO WELFARE ANALYTICS suite, including:

• Management of welfare activities within their establishment

• Creation, deployment, management, and archiving of welfare monitoring campaigns

• User rights management within an establishment

• Analysis of collected data

Data collected during campaigns are processed and stored on the AKONGO WELFARE CLOUD for each campaign, for backup, archiving, and analysis purposes. AKONGO undertakes not to disclose the results obtained by the user, except with the user's explicit consent. The data and results may be used for comparative analysis and/or scientific publications after anonymization.

• Access to the AKONGO questionnaire library.

AKONGO WELFARE MONITOR allows the user to administer questionnaires for analyzing the welfare of individuals and includes support information to assist the user in refining their assessments during different evaluation phases. Customized questionnaires (per species) are made available as part of the subscribed welfare support services from AKONGO.

At the end of the data collection operations, the collected data (question responses, behavioral observations, collected media data such as photos, videos, audio) are synchronized, processed, and archived on the AKONGO WELFARE CLOUD. Subsequently, this data and its analysis are accessible to the establishment's staff and AKONGO teams to fulfill their advisory mission. For comparative analysis purposes, the results (ratings) are aggregated. These anonymized data can be accessed by all users to refine their analysis. If an establishment does not wish for this data to be used for comparative analysis, a request must be made by email to contact@akongo.fr. Consequently, the establishment's access to comparative analysis features will be revoked, without affecting the proper functioning of the rest of the AKONGO WELFARE ANALYTICS suite.
